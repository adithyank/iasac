About Us

Kanchi Valluvan IAS Academy was inaugurated by Shri. A. Manikandan IAS (Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh) who successfully passed his IAS in Tamil Language.

This institute has been started in 2016 with the objectives of

1. Providing Excellent coaching for all competitive examinations at affordable prices
2. Providing the aspirants with Excellent Quality in Teaching and Updated study materials to achieve good results

Our Academy is successfully progressing in our journey in making hundreds of candidates achieve their future goals in the competitive examination streams.

We are expanding our wings by opening new centers in multiple cities in Tamil Nadu!

We are tirelessly working towards offering students an excellent coaching at affordable prices and lot of free offers to serve the society in the way that we know.

About the Founder

Mr. A. Naresh Kumar, Founder of Kanchi Valluvan IAS Academy is a passionate and young personality with B.A Tamil Literature. He has experience of 8 years in the academic side in the field of the competitive examinations in the areas of finding high quality faculties, most accurate setting of practice Exam Question papers. He himself cleared few of these competitive examinations.

He has a vision of spreading the high quality education with industry leading faculties in field of Competitive Examinations in both Urban and Rural areas at very affordable prices.

In order to efficiently implement his vision, he is partnering with the people at specific locations to administer the Institutes and manage the operations, of course with his academic strength in the field.
