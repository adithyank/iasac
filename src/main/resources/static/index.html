TNPSC (Group I/ G II, II-A, IV)

Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission exam is the name of the exam through which recruitments to government jobs in Tamil Nadu are made. The syllabus of this exam is according to the degree and qualification of the candidate. Some of the papers are also specific to the posts to which recruitments are made.

Exam includes the subjects General Knowledge, General Tamil, General English, subjects from Post Graduate Degree Standard, Degree Standard, Diploma Standard, Certificate Course, Intermediate Standard, HSC Standard and SSLC Standard.
